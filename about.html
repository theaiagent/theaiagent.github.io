Data Scientist

I am a data scientist focused on transforming messy, real-world datasets into clean, analysis-ready resources. My day-to-day work involves building automated cleaning pipelines, performing exploratory analysis, and creating reproducible workflows that scale.

With a strong foundation in Python and the modern data stack, I enjoy tackling the unglamorous but critical problems in data quality that make or break downstream analysis. When I am not wrangling data, I write about practical data science topics and share open-source projects that demonstrate real-world problem solving.
