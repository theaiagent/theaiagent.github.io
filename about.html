About Me

I am a Data Scientist and AI Engineer focused on building robust, production-ready systems that extract genuine value from complex, large-scale datasets. My work spans the full delivery lifecycle: raw data ingestion and cleaning, machine learning model development, LLM-powered application engineering, and end-to-end workflow automation.

On the AI engineering side, I design and evaluate LLM pipelines using frameworks such as AutoGen, CrewAI, and LangChain/LangGraph, build retrieval-augmented and agentic systems, and apply explainable AI methods (SHAP) to make model decisions transparent and auditable. I work across industries — wherever data quality, intelligent automation, or predictive capability can create measurable impact.

I also design and deploy no-code/low-code automation workflows using n8n, connecting APIs, databases, and AI models into seamless pipelines that eliminate manual bottlenecks. Whether it is standardising 125K rows of e-commerce data, orchestrating a multi-step AI agent, or wiring up a trigger-based n8n workflow, I care about correctness, reproducibility, and scale.
